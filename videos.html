Anaki Rocks The City 2013

March 2013
Two-day outdoor event for local Singaporean bands playing originals.

I was given the task of filming and producing a highlight video for the event.

Filmed on a Canon 550D/T2i with 18-55mm and 50mm f/1.8 lens.
Edited in Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects.

Service Project (Dining and Banquet Table Setup)

March 2015
A group project (team of 3) in our Mass Communications class, done as a service project for students in a Resort Management class.

We were tasked with shooting a video for students from the other class. We handled the technical aspect of the shoots, while the script and performance was done by students in the other class.

Filmed on a Panasonic AC-AG160 and a Canon 550D/T2i with 18-55mm and 50mm f/1.8 lens.
Edited in Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects.

Accessibility at Methodist University

April 2015
Group project (team of 4) - Short documentary on how Methodist University accomodates students with disabilities with help from the Accessibility Resources department.

Special thanks to the people that agreed to an interview with us.

Filmed on a Panasonic AC-AG160 and a Canon 550D/T2i with 18-55mm and 50mm f/1.8 lens.
Edited in Adobe Premiere Pro and Adobe After Effects.
